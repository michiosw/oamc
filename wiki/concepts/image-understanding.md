# Definition
Image understanding is a model's ability to interpret, generate, and utilize visual content (such as photos, icons, and mood boards) within the design or reasoning process.

# Key Ideas
- Visual asset selection (style, content, mood)
- Visual reasoning in support of hierarchy, atmosphere, and brand

# Variants
- Mood board usage
- Direct image generation versus image search

# Open Questions
- How to measure success in visual reasoning for LLMs?
