# Definition
Narrative structure in frontend design refers to organizing a page into a clear, goal-driven sequence (hero, support, detail, CTA), guiding the user's attention and actions.

# Key Ideas
- Hero section establishes brand/promise
- Supporting imagery/context
- Product details and credibility
- Final CTA focuses user on next action

# Variants
- Linear vs. branching narratives
- Marketing vs. product UI narratives

# Open Questions
- Are certain narrative structures better for brand recall versus conversion?
