# Definition
Frontend design concerns the visual and interaction portions of websites and applications, focusing on how users experience and operate digital products.

# Key Ideas
- Restraint balanced with invention
- Reliance on established patterns, but allowance for new, brand-specific invention
- Expressive imagery, layout, and typography
- Narrative structure underlying section order and content flow
- Rigorous constraint via design systems and visual references

# Variants
- Landing pages
- Apps/dashboards
- Games and interactive experiences

# Open Questions
- How hard should design rules be enforced for creativity?
- How do models balance overfitting to common patterns versus executing novel designs?
