# Definition
A mood board is a curated collection of visual references—images, colors, type, and more—that helps define the desired look and feel of a project.

# Key Ideas
- Guides visual direction of generated UIs
- Can be created by a model or supplied by humans

# Variants
- Designer-supplied vs. AI-generated

# Open Questions
- Can automated mood boards match expert-curated ones in effectiveness?
